Policy Change Notice

Our API Compatibility Policy has been revised in this release. Our old policy was designed with much shorter release cycles in mind. In practice it forced us to keep irrelevant APIs for many years, slowing down innovation and new development.

The new policy addresses that and allows us to have shorter deprecation and removal periods.

We expect to keep this new policy unchanged at least until the next major release.
